Real-time E-commerce App

Overview

GitHub-Lnk: https://github.com/KunkaiLin1205/E-commerce.git

Developed a Full Stack E-commerce application with Angular frontend and Spring Boot backend integration

Implemented the backend to search products by categories or keywords and track previous orders for login users using REST APIs and MySQL database

Built a shopping cart that can process checkout by Reactive forms and Stripe APIs

Above is the product list of the website. You can search the products by categories at left side or keywords using the search bar at top.

The product lists are paginated and you can modify the page size youself.

Click the title of each product, you will be nevigated to the detail view of that product. To add the product to cart, you can click "Add to cart".

In your shopping cart, you can add/remove items and modify the quantity of each item.

To process cart checkout, clicking "Checkout" at bottom.

To complete the checkout, filling out the information needed and the summary of order is displayed at the bottom.

The order information will be stored to the database for login users, and your order tracking number will be returned.
